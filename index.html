About Us

Belhekar College of Agriculture, Nevasa, and its parent Belhekar Group Of Institutes, established by Sulochana Belhekar Samajik va Bahuddeshiya Shikshan Sanstha, are committed to empowering rural students through quality agricultural, technical, and professional education. Located in Bhanashiware, the institute bridges traditional farming practices with modern technologies, fostering community development, sustainable farming, and youth empowerment to nurture the next generation of professionals.
